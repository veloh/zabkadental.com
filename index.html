About Us

Welcome to Keridam Dental Clinic

Keridam Dental Clinic, a leading limited company established in 2016, is at the forefront of delivering exceptional dental care on a global scale. Located in the heart of Nairobi’s central business district at Kenwood House, the clinic prioritizes meeting the diverse dental needs of its patients, both locally and internationally. With a significant percentage of international clientele accessing their services through various channels, including online platforms, referrals, and word of mouth, Keridam Dental Clinic maintains a strong focus on ensuring patient confidentiality and privacy to foster trust and long-lasting relationships.

Where your smile is our priority. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, our clinic is dedicated to providing top-notch dental care in a warm and welcoming environment.

At Keridam, we understand that a healthy smile is more than just good oral hygiene it’s a reflection of your overall well-being. That’s why our team of experienced dentists and specialists are committed to delivering personalized care tailored to your unique needs. Why Keridam?

Your Dentist Knows Best

Dont rush when you brush!

Keridam Dental Clinic professionals generally recommend at least 2 minutes of brushing, though with an appropriate technique, yet in the general population, the average brushing time is closer to 45 seconds.

Visit your dentist once in 6 months

Regular dental visits are important because they help to keep your teeth and gums healthy. They also enable your dentist to catch diseases in their earliest stages.

Dont Forget to Floss!

Dental floss is an affordable, easy-to-use product that can help you maintain healthy oral care and prevent bad breath. Flossing is a good habit to get into that most people find pleasurable and satisfying.
